A paragraph (from the Ancient Greek παράγραφος, parágraphos, " to write beside ") is a self-contained unit of discourse in writing dealing with a particular point or idea. A paragraph consists of one or more sentences. Paragraph definition is - a subdivision of a written composition that consists of one or more sentences, deals with one point or gives the words of one speaker, and begins on a new usually indented line.
